MAROON 5 FAN PAGE

Bio

Maroon 5 is an american pop rock and funk pop band from Los Angeles, California.

The members of the band came together as KARA'S FLOWERS in 1994, while they were still in High School.

Maroon 5 has sold more than 120 million records, making them one of the World's best-selling music Artists.

Members of the Band:

  • Adam Levine
  • Jesse Carmichael
  • Mickey Madden
  • James Valentine
  • Matt Flynn
  • PJ Morton
  • Sam Farrar
